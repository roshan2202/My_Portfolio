01 / About

From molecule
to medicine.

I build at the intersection of vaccine science, genomics and molecular pharmacology — where sequence data becomes therapeutic candidates and biology becomes code.

Trained in B.Pharm at Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Pharmacy, I've pivoted my focus toward translational vaccine research, genomic target identification and AI-assisted bioinformatics pipelines. My fascination lives in the messy in-between — where toxicology meets target validation and a genome annotation becomes a lead compound.

The thread across all of it is rigour applied where impact is decided: a vaccine only matters if it's manufacturable, immunogenic and equitably accessible. I work toward that end — from antigen design and carrier systems through to biotech-scale formulation and safety modelling.
